Privacy

You'd be surprised how little I care about you: I do not store any information about you, nor do I place cookies or try to track you in any other way. However, I have some online games I made using Flash that will store a special kind of cookie that belong to Flash software that is used to save your game.

If you message me, I will try to keep it private. Of course, if it is regarding a bomb plot, etc., I am obliged to tell officials.

I am not currently monetising the website, however should this change, the advertising company (e.g. Google) will have their own stipulations and will then require the use of cookies, and will store information about you. But as of now, it's a moot point.

External links (like the two below) are just that: external. This means I have no control over them and accept no responsibility regarding their content. If I use affiliation links, I will explicitly state them. At the moment I do not have any on the website.

As a side note, if privacy is a conern for you, I would encourage you to investigate privacy software. The two free software I highly recommend are: Ghostery and uBlock.

Ghostery can prevent many tracking cookies and uBlock prevents the loading of advertisement. Do remember to whitelist sites you wish to support however as many rely on the income of advertising to operate.
